About Me

Zuming Huang is a senior algorithm engineer at the multi-modality cognition team of Ant Group since April 2019. Previously he served as an algorithm engineer at Baidu Inc., department of computer vision technology. He received his Bachelor's degree in Information Engineering at South China University of Technology (SCUT), Master's degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligence System from Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA). His research interests include OCR, Document Intelligence and Large Multimodal Models.
