About Me

Zuming Huang received his Bachelor's degree in Information Engineering at South China University of Technology (SCUT), Master's degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligence System from Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA). He is a Senior Algorithm Engineer at Ant Group since April 2019. His research interests include OCR and document image understanding.
